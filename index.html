About me

Hey there. My name is Joseff and I'd like to thank you for visiting my little part of the Internet.

I'll apologize in advance for the brevity of this site; the hardest part of a personal website for me is the content, which is wild because in real life I'm quite the talker.

I’ve been a programmer, a project manager and an entrepreneur. Different experiences are a good thing. Understanding who you are, and what you like to do is a good thing too.

I really wanted to do a fancy React or Angular personal site, but I just couldn't figure out what to build. So instead I just updated this bio site for you.

These days I build websites. Oh, I'm also writing a sci-fi novel too (1/2 done).

In 2012 I decided that development was more my style and shifted into the development life full time.

I love MMORPG's. I'm currently hip deep into Elder Scrolls Online (PS4: IreTheTiger).
