About me

Hello. My name is Joseff Betancourt and I'd like to thank you for visiting my little part of the Internet.

I’m a website programmer, a former certified project manager and an aspiring entrepreneur.

I build websites in PHP — mostly Wordpress, begrudgingly in Drupal, and always happy to build them in Fat Free Framework. I’m pretty handy in vanilla Javascript and know my way around JQuery. Bootstrap is my favorite CSS framework and I’m a big fan of SASS.

I’m also pretty handy with Amazon Web Services. I can build out your backend infrastructure using EC2, S3 and CloudFront. Anything more and we are talking about a big complicated project — something I’d love to sink my teeth into.

I’m a self thought programmer and currently the process of learning Node.JS, ReactJS, and Ruby. I’d love to find myself in a project that needs these technologies and even better if I can learn off of others in the process. If you’re planning on asking me a computer-science-class based question I kindly ask that you not bother with that; anyone can recite from a book.

In 2010 I graduated from Pace University with an Executive MBA (and MBA with 9 majors). At the time I was PMP Certified — a position I held for about 12 years before I decided that development was more my style.

In that vain, I’m a pretty process orientated manager. I can bring order and accountability to your organization with data based reporting and efficient problem resolution.
