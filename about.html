A breakaway moment occurred for me in 2007 when I decided to leave the government vendor world behind and head over to the publishing and advertising industries.

Finally around 2013 I left the Project Management track to pursue a career in Programming.

For the last year I've been an executive level team member for a travel news wire service doing hands-on PHP development -- making products that bring in the money.
